A couple of my top programming languages are C#, C++, C they are listed in order from most favorite to least.

If you want to see some of my work check out the links catagory next to this div. I have programmed since I was 12. It started off from modding mobile games, creating menus of cheats and general improvements to games that ran bad and etc. I used to run a YT page where I gave those cheats away for free and I gained quite a large following for the time 5K subscribers before youtube struck me down for copyright and being against TOS for reverse engineering and modding of copyrighted games. But that didnt stop me. They taught me alot about memory management and detection vectors for games. My good ol friend android mods taught me how to reverse engineer encrption on games after a while of doing it. It came really easy to me. After I was tired of mobile modding I went on to mod PC games and applications and general development on PC and thats where I am today! I am happy to be where I am and im not planning on stopping anytime soon.
